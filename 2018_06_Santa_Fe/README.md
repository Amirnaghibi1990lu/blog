I was in [Santa Fe](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Santa_Fe,_New_Mexico) last week for a conference, it is really beautiful there, even on the [train from Albuquerque](https://www.riometro.org/rio-metro-schedules/train-schedule/train-weekday) (highly recommend to take the train, cheap, relax and beautiful). I also visited University of New Mexico, thanks to [Brandon](http://epswww.unm.edu/faculty-and-staff/schmandt/) and [Chengxin](https://www.researchgate.net/profile/Chengxin_Jiang), who hosted me there, I had a great time at UNM. Here are some pictures I took, and enjoy it!
